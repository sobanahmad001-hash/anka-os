# Anka OS — Phase 5 Marketing Environment

## Marketing Identity
Marketing is the campaign and content execution environment of Anka OS.

## Marketing Purpose
Marketing helps the team manage campaigns, content pipeline, approvals, publishing flow, performance context, and execution coordination.

## Marketing Owns
- active campaigns
- content pipeline
- approvals
- publishing flow
- linked assets/research
- performance notes
- marketing-specific blockers

## Marketing Core Views
1. Marketing Overview
2. Campaigns
3. Content Pipeline
4. Approvals
5. Calendar & Publishing
6. Performance & Insights

## Marketing Should Not Be
- just a calendar
- just a posting board
- just an analytics wrapper
- just a content storage area

## Marketing AI Role
Marketing AI is a campaign and content coordination helper.

It helps with:
- summarize campaign status
- organize content priorities
- explain bottlenecks
- draft updates
- summarize approvals state
- connect work to outcomes
- surface weak points in pipeline

# Anka OS — Phase 4 Design Environment

## Design Identity
Design is the creative execution environment of Anka OS.

## Design Purpose
Design helps the team manage briefs, deliverables, review cycles, revisions, handoff, and creative execution context.

## Design Owns
- active design projects
- briefs and deliverables
- feedback cycles
- revision state
- handoff readiness
- linked assets/context
- design-specific blockers

## Design Core Views
1. Design Overview
2. Briefs & Deliverables
3. Review Cycles
4. Feedback & Revisions
5. Handoff
6. Assets & Context

## Design Should Not Be
- just a Figma wrapper
- just a review board
- just a moodboard/asset browser
- just a generic creative dashboard

## Design AI Role
Design AI is a creative coordination helper.

It helps with:
- summarize briefs
- organize feedback
- identify repeated revision patterns
- highlight unclear requests
- prepare handoff summaries
- track deliverable context
- surface stalled reviews

# Phase 12 — Design + Marketing + AI Wiring

## Goal
Map Design and Marketing into the new environment model and define environment-aware AI wiring.

## Design Direction
Design becomes:
- brief-first
- review-cycle-aware
- revision-aware
- handoff-aware
- asset/context aware

## Likely Design Work
- map design review logic into Design environment
- map moodboard/assets into support context
- shape design overview, reviews, feedback, handoff

## Marketing Direction
Marketing becomes:
- campaign-first
- content-pipeline aware
- approval-aware
- publishing-aware
- performance-aware

## Likely Marketing Work
- map campaigns into Campaigns view
- map content into pipeline
- map reports into performance context
- shape overview, approvals, publishing flow

## AI Wiring Direction

### Admin AI
- org summary
- risks
- bottlenecks
- leadership summaries

### Development AI
- technical blockers
- recent changes
- work breakdown
- release/PR context

### Design AI
- brief summary
- feedback grouping
- revision patterns
- handoff summary

### Marketing AI
- campaign status
- content priorities
- approval bottlenecks
- outcome-linked summaries

## Test After Phase 12
- Design and Marketing routes align to shell
- environment identity is clear
- AI prompts/behavior differ by environment
- no generic chatbot behavior in environment-specific flows

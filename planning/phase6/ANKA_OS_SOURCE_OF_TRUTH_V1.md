# Anka OS — Source of Truth v1

## Product Definition
Anka OS is an organizational operating system for internal execution, built around project management as the primary core, with Admin as the organizational spine and specialized environments for Development, Design, and Marketing.

## Core Product Structure
### Organizational Spine
- Admin

### Shared Core
- organizations
- departments
- teams
- users / profiles
- roles / permissions
- projects
- project_members
- tasks
- task_dependencies
- project_updates
- blockers
- decisions
- documents
- rules
- ai_project_memory

### Specialized Environments
- Development
- Design
- Marketing

## Product Principles
- project management is primary
- AI is secondary but highly important
- AI is specialized per environment
- environments are not separate products
- admin provides cross-functional control and visibility
- the OS should support your own organization, not generic SaaS assumptions

## Environment Summary

### Admin
Purpose:
organizational visibility, oversight, coordination, and control

### Development
Purpose:
technical execution, sprint flow, blockers, releases, technical decisions

### Design
Purpose:
briefs, reviews, revisions, handoff, creative execution

### Marketing
Purpose:
campaigns, content pipeline, approvals, publishing, performance context

## AI Summary
### Admin AI
strategic and oversight-heavy

### Development AI
technical and execution-focused

### Design AI
creative coordination and feedback-focused

### Marketing AI
campaign and content coordination focused

## Design Direction
Anka OS should not look or behave like:
- Notion
- Asana
- Monday
- desktop/workspace shell
- generic third-party app launcher

Anka OS should behave like:
- a focused internal organizational operating system
